🪙 Pecunia — The Atlas of Money

Pecunia is an interactive atlas of monetary history, tracing the evolution of money from ancient barter systems to modern digital currencies. Explore how currencies, financial innovations, and economic ideas spread across civilizations and continents.

Use the timeline to travel through 5,000 years of monetary history. Click markers to learn about individual currencies. Switch lenses to view different aspects — from innovation and trade to crises and regulation.

Part of The Pangea Project — exploring the interconnected history of human civilization through interactive maps.
