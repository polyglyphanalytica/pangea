About Lex

Lex — The Atlas of Law & Justice is an interactive exploration of the world's legal systems, constitutions, codes, and milestones spanning over 4,000 years of human civilization.

From the Code of Hammurabi to modern AI regulation, Lex maps the transmission of legal concepts across cultures and centuries. Explore how ideas like habeas corpus, constitutional governance, and human rights evolved and spread across the globe.

Use the timeline to travel through history, lenses to focus on specific aspects of each legal system, threads to trace the evolution of key legal concepts, and compare to examine systems side by side.

Part of The Pangea Project — a collection of interactive atlases exploring human civilization.
