⚔️ Bellum

The Atlas of War & Conflict — an interactive exploration of warfare throughout human history, spanning 5,000 years from the Bronze Age to the modern era.

Part of The Pangea Project, a collection of interactive atlases mapping the forces that shaped human civilization.

Navigate the timeline to see conflicts unfold across the ages. Use Lenses to examine wars through different analytical perspectives — causes, tactics, legacy, and more. Switch to Threads mode to trace how military innovations like gunpowder and blitzkrieg spread across conflicts. Use Compare mode to place two wars side by side.

Keyboard: Arrow keys to scrub timeline (Shift for larger steps), +/- to zoom, N/P to cycle wars, Space to play/pause, 0 to reset view.
