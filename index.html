100 Atlases
12 Themes

Each atlas maps a single domain of human knowledge onto an interactive timeline — from cosmic deep time to the present day. Scrub through billions of years of stellar evolution, millions of years of geological and biological change, or thousands of years of human invention. See which ideas travelled between civilisations, which discoveries made others possible, and whose contributions were written out of the story. Find the women history left out. This is not the encyclopaedia you'd expect. Or the atlas you'd expect. This is a new way to read the human story.
